WHAT THE ANIMATION SHOWS

  • Each moving dot is a packet of electrical energy (electron).
  • DC BLUE wires carry raw solar DC power from panels → inverter, and to/from the battery.
  • AC GREEN wires carry converted household AC electricity to your loads.
  • PURPLE dashed lines are Grid Feedback / Smart Meter signals.
  • Electrons appear only on active wires — if dots are flowing, power is moving.
  • When the RL agent picks CHARGE, electrons flow PV→BESS. When DISCHARGE, they flow BESS→Load.

WHAT'S RUNNING IN THE BACKEND

  • PowerGridworld — NREL physics engine simulating the IEEE 13 node feeder every 15 min.
  • EnergyStorageEnv — Models a 100 kWh BESS with charge/discharge efficiency.
  • SolarGridEnv — OpenEnv-compliant wrapper exposing Pydantic Observations & Actions.
  • RL Policy — A rule-based baseline agent: charge when Solar > Demand, else discharge.
  • Every 0.5 sec the backend pushes telemetry via WebSocket to this page.

WHAT EACH COMPONENT MEANS

  • PV Array — Solar panels generating DC electricity based on a sin-curve solar profile.
  • Combiner Box — Aggregates multiple panel strings into a single DC bus.
  • Bi-Dir Inverter — Converts DC←→AC. The RL brain's command gateway.
  • BESS — Battery stores excess solar. SOC% shown live from EnergyStorageEnv.
  • Smart Load — HVAC, EV, TV, Lights consuming AC power.
  • Utility Grid — External grid for import (when battery low) or export (when full).
  • Smart Meter — Measures net import/export. Drives the RL reward signal.
